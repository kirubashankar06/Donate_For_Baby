About us

We are the Smile Life Foundation. We are an Organisation that helps childrens and babies , Who are suffering from Nutritional Deficiency and are Malnourished by Birth and Due to Poor Living. We intitiated these services By 20th of July 1995. Almost we have completed 25 years doing this happiest Service all Over the world. On 1995 we started our first Headquarters Office in Chennai and Later we Opened Offices all over the World. We have our Offices in Countries like Africa,Turkey,Australia,USA,Japan,China,Bangladesh and in all the States of India etc. We have so far helped and gave hands to over 5,50,000 childrens and Babies who are Battling the Nutrional Deficiency such as Marasmus, Kwashiorkar, Polio and other ather Deficiencies etc., with the Help of Many Good Souls who joined hands with us. We first started our online site On 2010 to make the process of Donations Easier to all the people who love to Contribute. We would have not attained this level without the help of our Donors and Contributers . Finally, we would like to welcome you to our family and Join hand with us to help Childrens All over the World.
